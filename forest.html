Trails

01 Pictured Rocks  MI, US

Pictured Rocks lies within the northern hardwood-hemlock-white pine region of the eastern deciduous forest. About 80 percent of the lakeshore is covered by upland northern hardwoods.

I went there during this fall break. We hiked along the shores of sparkling Lake Superior and walked through tunnels of colorful maples and poplars. It was incredible!

02 Hallerbos Forest  Halle, Belgium

Always want to cross Hellerbos off my bucket list. Hellerbos is also known as blue forests, as in the spring the ground of this Belgian wood becomes covered with a carpet of bluebells. Short-distance hiking trails within 5 miles is accessible all year round, and puppies are also welcome!

03 Daxing'anling  Heilongjiang, China

Snowy hiking might be hard, but the breathtaking scenery will make it worthwhile. Daxing'anling is located at the north end of China. It is the only coniferous forest in China and the last biological gene bank of the cold temperate zone.
